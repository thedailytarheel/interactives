Differences between the Wainstein report and the narrative of the athletic-academic scandal
We’ve compared and contrasted the different narratives of UNC’s athletic-academic scandal against the Wainstein report. Here are the major categories of differences and what each different source says about them:
Home
Wainstein report Tutors wrote portions of papers for athletes and selected classes for athletes when they had no interest in doing so. Records obtained by the DTH Tutors wrote portions of papers, communicated with professors, and sometimes selected different classes for student-athletes than those they wanted to take. NCAA Notice of Allegations Said UNC failed to monitor the Academic Support Program for Student Athletes (ASPSA) where the tutors were employed
